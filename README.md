The Vehicle Insurance Dataset contains customer and vehicle-related information used to predict whether a customer is interested in purchasing vehicle insurance. It includes details such as age, gender, vehicle age, annual premium, driving license status, previous insurance history, and vehicle damage condition. The dataset consists of both numerical and categorical variables, making it suitable for Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), visualization, and machine learning projects. The target column, **Response**, indicates customer interest in insurance. This dataset helps insurance companies analyze customer behavior, improve marketing strategies, identify potential buyers, and develop predictive models for better business decision-making and sales growth.
